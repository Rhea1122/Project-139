AI Ping Pong











Instructions for the game



  1. Keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet from the laptop screen.
  3. Make sure your right wrist is visible on the camera.
  4. Two paddles will be visible, the red one is your paddle.
  5. A red dot will appear on your right wrist; move it in an ascending and descending manner.
  6. Press on the play button and enjoy the game!
